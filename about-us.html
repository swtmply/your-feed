About Us

YourFeed is a new type of Newsfeed, where in users can select their own themes and topics base on their mood. Through the mood selection they can control their feed for news, sports, showbiz, and weather reports.

With this new type of website, users can freely customize their feed base on the articles that they will be using. It certainly live up to its name as YourFeed

Our team is consists of 5 members that developed this system. Bacnis Henric, Calimlim Khen Chee, Lago Cyril, Marcial Alvaro, and Mendez Ian.
